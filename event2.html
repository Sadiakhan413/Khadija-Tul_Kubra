Transforming Spaces: Creative Classroom Makeover Day at Khadija Tul Kubra School

Khadijatul Kubra School recently hosted a vibrant and inspiring Creative Classroom Makeover Day, where students and teachers collaborated to transform their learning environments into imaginative and engaging spaces. Each classroom was given a unique theme, with colorful decorations, innovative layouts, and student-created artwork taking center stage. This hands-on event not only sparked creativity but also fostered a sense of ownership and pride among the students as they worked together to bring their ideas to life. The newly transformed classrooms now serve as dynamic spaces that enhance learning and inspire creativity.
