Celebrating Achievement: Khadija Tul Kubra School's Annual Day Awards Ceremony

Khadija Tul Kubra School recently celebrated its Annual Day, a momentous occasion where students' hard work and dedication throughout the year were recognized and rewarded. The event was marked by the announcement of academic results and the presentation of prizes to outstanding students in various categories. With a festive atmosphere, the ceremony featured inspiring speeches, heartfelt congratulations, and joyous celebrations. This special day not only honored individual achievements but also strengthened the sense of community and motivation within the school, as students, parents, and teachers came together to celebrate success and encourage future excellence.
